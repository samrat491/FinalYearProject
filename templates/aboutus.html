About This Project

Final Year Project • 3rd Year BIT Major

Welcome! This platform was built with a singular vision: to empower young people to explore and develop their creative writing skills. Writing a web novel doesn't require expensive equipment, a studio, or formal training—just a brain, a laptop, and an imagination.

This makes web novel writing the perfect creative outlet for introverts and dreamers who love spending their days in their rooms, constantly on their laptops, crafting entire universes from scratch.
